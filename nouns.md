


# Nouns

de, da- the

de- the (masculine)

da- the (feminine)

"a" and "an" don't excist

If you want to make noun from verb or adjective, you give ending "-ó" or -á" and remove ending "-oh" or ending "-e or -a"

E.G.:

rinalá- love

gaetó- the good

Last letter of noun discribes gender of this noun.

e, i, o, u- masculine

a,y- feminine
