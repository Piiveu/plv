
# Grammar

Plv has 2 genders- masculine and feminine, so it has 2 definitive articles- de(masculine) and da(feminine)

Plv hasn't got any indefinite articles.

All verbs ends with "-oh" 

E.G.: 

esoh- to be  

hoh- to have  

tunoh- to do
